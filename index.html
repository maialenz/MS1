About Us

Hello everyone! My name is Mai and this in the pictures is Sua, the main dog-boss behind Wet Paws. We created this company to help all human parents to keep our furry friends happy when our lives get too busy and we are not always able to be there at the right time to walk our dogs. This often makes us feel bad and we see that our dogs are not getting the level of exercise we want for them.
There are lots of situations where we have found ourselves in over the past years where we have needed the help of someone to take our dogs for a walk. Times when events have come up, we have had to work late, we have been on holiday or due to the pandemic, although we are spending longer at home, most of us are working much longer times than at the office. Or we just needed our dogs to burn some energy so they don't get anxious during that important skype meeting we might have.
We know what is to be in those situations, because we have been there. So we are here to help you help them when you can't. Seeing them happy is what makes us happy. Sua is looking forward to run and be best friends with your furry friend!
