About Us

Hello everyone! My name is Mai and this in the picture is Sua, the main dog-boss behind Wet Paws. We live in Bettystown, Ireland and we created this company to help all dog parents to keep our furry friends happy when our lives get too busy. Unfortunatelly we are not always able to be there for them, and it makes us sad not to be able to take them with us everywhere.

So if you ever had an event, extra work, holidays, an important online meeting, or you just needed some extra help burning your dogs extra energy (believe us, we know what's that), we are here to help you make them happy when life gets on your and your dog's way.

Seeing them happy is what makes us happy. And Sua is looking forward to run and be best friends with your furry friend!

Meet Us!

Would you like to meet Sua personally? Come to walk with us!

If before you decide to book our services you would like to meet us and introduce our dogs to each other, you can meet with us every day at 11.00 and 18.00 at the entrance of the Bettystown beach, located next to Bistro Restaurant. We well wait for 5 minutes before we start the beach walk if you want to join us and have a chat!

If you can't make it at those times or you rather talk privately, please fill our form with your details and send us an email so we can arrange a time to meet you at your most convinient time.
