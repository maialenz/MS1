OUR SERVICES

Dog walking

We will pick your dog up from your home* and they'll be brought for a fun walk. You can choose if you want your dog to be walked alone or in a group. The personalised experience means that your dog can enjoy their walk the way they want to around places they love (beach, park...). We will make sure your dog gets all the exercise and stimulation they need. It's also a great way to socialize your puppy!.

**(At this moment we are only able to provide this services around Bettystown area, but we are working on expanding our services to more areas, so keep an eye!)

Dog sitting

This is a perfect choice for giving your dog additional enrichment during the day, or maybe you need your puppy to get some additional socialisation while they are young. The personalised experience we provide means that your dog can enjoy their day in a relaxed and safe, home environment. We will be with them from when you leave until you come back. This is a much better alternative to crowded commercial dog daycare centres.

**(We only look after one household at the time. This is an individualized service. We only take one booking per day, so your dog will have all our attention and love)

Ask about this service

Boarding

Do you need someone to look after your dog while you are away on holidays/work/events/ for more than one day? Your dog stays overnight at our house and enjoys personalised dog care, during day and night.Home dog boarding is suitable for dogs of all ages, including puppies. This is a more safe and loving alternative to a commercial dog kennel or dog hotel.

**(We only offer this service to one family per night (1-2 dogs) unless specified otherwise. Please ask about our availability in advance)
